Over 85% of Australian National University graduates now access their official academic transcripts through the My eQuals digital platform, according to the university’s 2026 Student Administration Report. This shift toward secure, verifiable digital credentials has streamlined the document request process for both current students and alumni across 100+ countries. Whether you need an ANU academic transcript for employment verification, further study applications, or professional licensing, understanding the correct request pathway saves you time and avoids processing delays. This guide walks you through every step, from determining which transcript type you need to receiving your final document.

## Understanding ANU Transcript Types and Their Uses

An **ANU official transcript** is a comprehensive record of all courses undertaken, grades achieved, and qualifications conferred during your enrolment at the Australian National University. The university issues several distinct transcript formats depending on your purpose and graduation status.

**Digital transcripts** issued through **My eQuals ANU** represent the standard format for most requests. These documents carry encrypted digital signatures and can be shared securely with third parties such as employers, other universities, or migration authorities. Once issued, a digital transcript remains accessible indefinitely through your My eQuals account.

**Hard copy transcripts** remain available for graduates who completed their studies before 2018 or for specific international requirements where physical documents are mandatory. ANU Student Administration processes these on security paper with official stamps and signatures.

For students who have not yet graduated, an **interim transcript** shows completed coursework to date. This document proves useful for scholarship applications, exchange program requirements, or job applications during your final semester.

**AHEGS**, the Australian Higher Education Graduation Statement, accompanies final transcripts for graduates from 2011 onward. This supplementary document provides contextual information about the Australian qualifications framework and ANU’s institutional standing.

## Eligibility Requirements and Account Access

Before initiating an **ANU academic transcript request**, confirm your access to the relevant university systems. Current students and recent graduates typically maintain active credentials for the ANU Identity Management System.

**Current students** log into ISIS, the Interactive Student Information System, using their university ID and password. From ISIS, you can navigate directly to the transcript request portal or access My eQuals for previously issued documents. If you encounter login difficulties, ANU IT Services provides 24/7 support through the Service Desk portal.

**Former students** who completed studies after 2016 generally retain My eQuals access through the personal email address they registered before graduation. If you cannot locate your My eQuals invitation or have changed email providers, contact ANU Student Administration with your former student ID and date of birth for account recovery.

Graduates from **before 2016** may not have automatic My eQuals access. In these cases, you must submit a manual transcript request through the ANU Alumni document portal. Processing times for historical records average 10 to 15 business days, as physical verification of archived records may be necessary.

**Third-party requests**—such as background check agencies or employers seeking verification—must include signed authorisation from the student or graduate. ANU does not release academic records without explicit consent, complying with Australian privacy legislation.

## Step-by-Step: Requesting via My eQuals for Current Students

The My eQuals platform handles the majority of **ANU official transcript** requests for enrolled students. Follow these steps to order your documents efficiently.

**Step 1: Log into ISIS.** Navigate to the ANU homepage and select “Current Students,” then click through to ISIS using your university credentials. The ISIS dashboard displays your enrolment summary, fee statements, and academic history.

**Step 2: Navigate to Academic Records.** Within ISIS, locate the “Academic Records” or “Graduation” tile. Select “Order Official Documents” from the dropdown menu. This action redirects you to the ANU payment gateway and document ordering system.

**Step 3: Select document type.** Choose “Official Academic Transcript” from the available options. The system prompts you to confirm whether you need a digital copy only or both digital and hard copy versions. **Digital-only transcripts** cost AUD 35 as of the 2026 fee schedule, while combined digital and hard copy orders total AUD 50.

**Step 4: Confirm delivery details.** For digital delivery, ensure your My eQuals account email is current. The system sends an automated notification when your transcript is ready—typically within **2 to 3 business days** for standard requests. Hard copies ship via Australia Post standard mail domestically or international airmail for overseas addresses.

**Step 5: Complete payment.** ANU accepts Visa, Mastercard, and BPAY for transcript orders. Once payment processes, you receive an order confirmation with a reference number. Retain this number for any follow-up inquiries with Student Administration.

## How Former Students Order Transcripts Without ISIS Access

If you graduated or discontinued studies more than 12 months ago, your ISIS access likely expired. ANU provides alternative pathways for **ANU graduation documents** and transcript requests.

**Alumni with My eQuals access** can log directly into the My eQuals platform using their registered email. Check your inbox for the original “My eQuals Registration” message from ANU. Once logged in, select “Request New Document” and follow the prompts to order an updated transcript reflecting your complete academic history.

**Alumni without My eQuals access** should visit the ANU Alumni website and locate the “Document Request” form. This web form collects your full name at time of study, student ID (if known), date of birth, program of study, and approximate years of attendance. Providing accurate details accelerates the verification process.

For **urgent requests**, ANU offers a priority processing service at an additional AUD 25 fee. Priority orders received before 12:00 PM Canberra time on business days ship or become available digitally within **1 business day**. Standard orders from former students typically take **5 to 8 business days**.

Graduates who completed studies at the **ANU College of Law** or **ANU Medical School** should note that professional accreditation documentation may require separate application through the relevant faculty office. Law graduates seeking admission to practice certificates, for example, often need both a standard transcript and a letter of completion from the ANU Legal Workshop.

## Troubleshooting Common Transcript Request Issues

Even straightforward **ANU academic transcript request** processes occasionally encounter obstacles. Knowing how to address common problems saves frustration and unnecessary delays.

**Missing grades or incomplete records** occur when final results have not been officially released. Check the ANU Academic Calendar for result publication dates each semester. If your transcript omits recently completed courses beyond the expected release date, contact your course convenor to confirm that marks have been submitted to the Examinations Office.

**Name changes** between your time of study and transcript request require supporting documentation. Submit a certified copy of your marriage certificate, deed poll, or passport showing both your former and current names to Student Administration before placing your order. Processing name change verifications adds **3 to 5 business days**.

**Account lockouts** from My eQuals typically resolve through the platform’s password reset function. If your registered email is no longer accessible, you must contact ANU directly with alternative contact details and proof of identity. The university’s Service Desk cannot reset My eQuals passwords without identity verification for security reasons.

**Payment failures** often stem from bank security blocks on transactions classified as international. ANU’s payment processor operates from Australia; some overseas banks flag this as potentially fraudulent. Contact your bank to authorise the transaction, then retry through the ANU payment portal.

## Document Verification and Sharing Your ANU Transcript

Digital transcripts through **My eQuals ANU** offer robust verification features that employers and institutions increasingly prefer over paper documents.

Each digital transcript carries an encrypted digital signature verifiable through the My eQuals platform. Recipients can click the “Verify” button on your shared document to confirm its authenticity instantly. This eliminates the need for notarised copies or sealed envelope delivery—a common requirement for hard copy transcripts.

To share your transcript, log into My eQuals and select the document you wish to share. Enter the recipient’s email address and set an access expiry date if desired. The recipient receives a secure link valid for the period you specify. **You can revoke access at any time** through your My eQuals dashboard.

For organisations that require **direct verification from ANU**, Student Administration provides a third-party verification service. Employers or background check agencies can submit verification requests with your signed consent form. ANU responds to these inquiries within **5 business days** and charges a AUD 25 verification fee per document.

Hard copy transcripts intended for international use may require an **Apostille or Authentication** stamp from the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade. ANU does not provide this service directly; you must forward your sealed transcript to DFAT after receiving it. Plan for an additional **2 to 3 weeks** for this process.

## FAQ

### Q: How long does an ANU academic transcript take to process in 2026?

Standard digital transcript orders through My eQuals process within **2 to 3 business days** for current students. Former students without active ISIS access should allow **5 to 8 business days** for verification. Priority processing, available for an additional AUD 25, reduces turnaround to **1 business day** if submitted before 12:00 PM Canberra time. Peak periods such as November through February may extend standard processing to **5 business days**.

### Q: Can I request an ANU transcript if I graduated 20 years ago?

Yes. ANU maintains academic records dating back to the university’s founding in 1946. Graduates from before 2016 who lack My eQuals access must submit a manual request through the ANU Alumni document portal. Processing for historical records takes **10 to 15 business days** because physical archive retrieval may be necessary. Providing your full name at time of study, student ID or date of birth, and program details accelerates this process.

### Q: What is the difference between an ANU transcript and an AHEGS?

An **ANU official transcript** lists all courses attempted, grades received, and qualifications conferred. The **Australian Higher Education Graduation Statement (AHEGS)** provides contextual information including the Australian Qualifications Framework level, ANU’s institutional profile, and graduate attributes. Graduates from 2011 onward receive an AHEGS automatically with their final transcript. A standalone AHEGS costs AUD 20 digitally or AUD 35 with a hard copy as of the 2026 fee schedule.

### Q: How do I share my ANU transcript with a potential employer?

Log into **My eQuals ANU**, select the transcript you wish to share, enter the employer’s email address, and set an access expiry period. The employer receives a secure link to view and verify your document. This digital sharing method meets verification requirements for **over 90% of Australian employers** and most international organisations. If the employer insists on a hard copy, order a paper transcript through the standard request process and allow **3 to 7 business days** for domestic shipping.
